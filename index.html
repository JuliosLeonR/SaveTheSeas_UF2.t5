Save The Seas

SaveTheSeas is a foundation dedicated to protecting and conserving the world's seas and oceans. The organization focuses on projects aimed at cleaning up plastics and other waste from marine environments, promoting sustainability, and raising awareness about the importance of reducing pollution. Additionally, it supports initiatives to preserve marine biodiversity by aiding endangered species and collaborating with coastal communities to implement responsible practices. The foundation combines local actions with global campaigns to create a positive and lasting impact on marine ecosystems.
